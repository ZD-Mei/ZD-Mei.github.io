About

I am now a Postdoctoral Research Fellow co-mentored by Dong D. Wang and Curtis Huttenhower. at Brigham and Women's Hospital and Harvard Medical School. I have a blended research background in Epidemiology, Bioinformatics, Genetics, and Nutrition.

My research is dedicated to integrating lifestyle and environmental factors with multi-omics data-including but not limited to genomics, transcriptomics, proteomics, gut metagenomics, and metabolomics-to explore various aspects of human health, such as cardiometabolic diseases, cognitive decline, and cancer, within both observation and intervention studies. Specifically, my objectives are to:

1. Identify lifestyle and environmental factors linked to human health

  • Qi W, Mei Z, Sun Z, Lin C, Lin J, Li J, Ji JS, Zheng Y. Exposure to Multiple Air Pollutants and the Risk of Fractures: A Large Prospective Population-Based Study. J Bone Miner Res. 2023 Nov;38(11):1549-1559.
  • Hu J, Zhao M, Lin C, Sun Z, Chen GC, Mei Z, Zheng Y. Associations of visceral adipose tissue with bone mineral density and fracture: observational and Mendelian randomization studies. Nutr Metab (Lond). 2022 Jul 12;19(1):45.

2. Discover biomarkers and predictors for diseases

  • Mei Z, Dong X, Qian Y, Hong D, Xie Z, Yao G, Qin A, Gao S, Hu J, Liang L, Zheng Y, Su J. Association between the metabolome and bone mineral density in a Chinese population. EBioMedicine. 2020 Dec;62:103111.
  • Lin C, Sun Z, Mei Z, Zeng H, Zhao M, Hu J, Xia M, Huang T, Wang C, Gao X, Zheng Y. The causal associations of circulating amino acids with blood pressure: a Mendelian randomization study. BMC Med. 2022 Oct 28;20(1):414.

3. Decipher the mechanism underlying the association of risk factors with diseases and validate these findings through in vivo and in vitro studies (open to collaboration)

  • Mei Z, Chen GC, Wang Z, Usyk M, Yu B, Baeza YV, Humphrey G, Benitez RS, Li J, Williams-Nguyen JS, Daviglus ML, Hou L, Cai J, Zheng Y, Knight R, Burk RD, Boerwinkle E, Kaplan RC, Qi Q. Dietary factors, gut microbiota, and serum trimethylamine-N-oxide associated with cardiovascular disease in the Hispanic Community Health Study/Study of Latinos. Am J Clin Nutr. 2021 Jun 1;113(6):1503-1514.

4. Generate scientific evidence supporting precision medicine

  • Mei Z, Chen GC, Hu J, Lin C, Sun Z, Liu C, Geng X, Yuan C, Qi Q, Zheng Y. Habitual use of fish oil supplements, genetic predisposition, and risk of fractures: a large population-based study. Am J Clin Nutr. 2021 Sep 1;114(3):945-954.

5. Pinpoint potential targets for intervention
