About

I am now a Postdoctoral Research Fellow co-mentored by Dong D. Wang and Curtis Huttenhower. at Brigham and Women's Hospital and Harvard Medical School. I have a blended research background in Epidemiology, Bioinformatics, Genetics, and Nutrition.

My research is dedicated to integrating lifestyle and environmental factors with multi-omics data-including but not limited to genomics, transcriptomics, proteomics, gut metagenomics, and metabolomics-to explore various aspects of human health, such as cardiometabolic diseases, cognitive decline, and cancer, within both observation and intervention studies.
